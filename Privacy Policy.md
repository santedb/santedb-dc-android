## **APP NAME** Privacy Policy

**APP NAME** "the App” provides discrete medical records tracking for clinical service delivery "the Service" 
to clinicians working with the **Ministry of Health** and/or sanctioned **Third Party Organizations**. This Privacy Policy describes how personal information is collected, used, and shared when you install or use the App in connection within your clinic.

## Personal Information the App Collects

When you install the App, the **Ministry of Health** are automatically able to access certain types of information from your clinic: 

* The names/profiles of users within your clinic
* The types of devices being used and/or their security parameters
* Data from the device's camera for the purpose of scanning barcodes
* Security audit trail information (actions, timestamps, etc.)

Additionally, we collect the following types of personal information from you and/or your patients once you have installed the App: 

* Demographics information including:
  * Name
  * Date of Birth
  * Gender
  * Residence(s)
  * Places of Birth and Death
  * Employer and School Attendence Information
* Personal Identifiers including:
  * National Health Identifiers
  * National Citizen Registration Identifiers
  * Drivers License & Credit Card Numbers
* Information related to clinical service delivery including (but not limited to):
  * Substance Administrations (administration of medications)
  * Observations (such as height, weight, etc.)
  * Procedures & Encounters (such as hospital stays, etc.)
  * Invoicing Events (such as billing & payment information)
